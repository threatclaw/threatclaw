source. It is inspired by the Apache Individual Contributor License
Agreement (ICLA v2.2) and the Harmony Agreements CA-Individual (outbound

ThreatClaw's dual-license model and international contributor base.

- **"Project"** — the ThreatClaw software in its Community Edition
  (AGPL v3) and its Commercial Edition, plus any associated websites,

- **"Contribution"** — any work of authorship (code, documentation,

  submitting the Contribution. If You are submitting on behalf of an
  employer or other entity, "You" includes that entity, which You warrant
  has authorized You to act on its behalf.

## How to Sign

Signing is automated via the CLA Assistant bot on GitHub:

1. Submit a pull request to the ThreatClaw repository
2. The CLA Assistant bot comments on your PR with a link
3. Click the link and sign with your GitHub account (one click)
4. The bot updates your PR status and records your signature

You only need to sign once per CLA version. The signature applies
to all future Contributions until the CLA is revised.
